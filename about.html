About Us

Fleeti is a brand backed by its local and international carriers who help people move their items with a peace of mind . Whether moving an item within the city, country or across borders we always aim to deliver great experiences.

We believe in personalization and therefore we offer a variety of services by vetted and top-rated carriers.During the move, we aim to provide a great overall experience for our customers by ensuring that the logistics flow is smooth and laminar. Specializing in offering our clients a diverse hub during any transport, is the pivot of our success.

Great experiences that are rapid, safe and simple is our cutting edge. At Fleeti our motto is “if there is a will there is away “ and therefore, we always find creative ways to enhance our customer experience.
